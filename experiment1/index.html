Experiment 2 - Vector Art, Animation & Interactivity

Description

In this experiment, I am trying to replicate what I see when I hear "The Line" by Twenety One Pilots. A song that was used for the show "Arcane" and what I used to decide on the colors for my art piece.

Technical

The code was seperated into two files for me to easily edit the functions that I had created. I created a Mold file to easily edit how the lines looked and behavior of my art worked. I also defined my curve lines in a seperate function that is toggled when the mouse is clicked. Overall, the technial side of the art piece is very straightforward and can be seen on my github and documentation.

Reflection

I was the only person who worked on this code. I enjoyed the process of how I got to my end result, especially as someone who is still constantly learning how to code and to develop me JavaScript skills. There was a lot of trial and error and scoping to finish this assignment in a decent amount of time.
